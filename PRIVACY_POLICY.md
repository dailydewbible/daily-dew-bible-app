# Privacy Policy for Daily Dew Bible App

**Last Updated:** December 20, 2025  
**Effective Date:** December 20, 2025

## Introduction

Daily Dew Bible App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Daily Dew Bible App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** - Used for authentication and account recovery
- **User ID** - Automatically generated unique identifier

### 2. Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the app:
- **App usage statistics** - Features used, time spent, interactions
- **Device information** - Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Crash reports** - Technical data when the app crashes to help us fix bugs

### 3. User-Generated Content

Content you create within the app:
- **Bible notes** - Stored locally on your device with AES-256 encryption
- **Highlighted verses** - Stored in our secure database
- **Reading plans** - Stored locally on your device with AES-256 encryption
- **Bible chat conversations** - Stored locally on your device with AES-256 encryption and automatically deleted after 24 hours (unless marked as permanent)

### 4. Subscription Information

When you purchase a subscription:
- **Subscription status** - Managed through RevenueCat and Apple's App Store
- **Credit balance** - Number of credits available for premium features
- **Transaction history** - Handled by Apple's payment system (we do not store payment card information)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **Providing Services**
   - User authentication and account management
   - Delivering Bible content and features
   - Processing subscription purchases
   - Managing credit system for premium features

2. **Improving the App**
   - Analyzing usage patterns to improve features
   - Identifying and fixing bugs
   - Developing new features based on user needs

3. **Communication**
   - Sending important updates about the app
   - Responding to support requests
   - Notifying about subscription status changes

4. **Security**
   - Detecting and preventing fraud
   - Protecting against unauthorized access
   - Ensuring data integrity

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

- **Data Collected:** Email, user ID, device info, usage data, crash logs

**Important:** We do not send your personal notes, highlights, or private conversations to any third-party service. These remain encrypted on your device.

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage (On Your Device)
- **Bible notes** - Encrypted with AES-256
- **Reading plans** - Encrypted with AES-256
- **Bible chat conversations** - Encrypted with AES-256, auto-deleted after 24 hours
- **App preferences** - Stored locally

### Cloud Storage (Firebase)
- **User account information** - Email, user ID
- **Highlighted verses** - Verse references and categories
- **Credit balance** - Number of credits available
- **Subscription status** - Current subscription tier

### Security Measures
- All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS
- Local data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- API keys are stored server-side and never exposed in the app
- Regular security audits and updates

## Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained until you delete your account
- **Bible chat conversations:** Automatically deleted after 24 hours (unless marked as permanent)
- **Usage analytics:** Retained for up to 2 years
- **Crash reports:** Retained for up to 90 days
- **Notes and reading plans:** Retained until you delete them or delete your account

## Your Rights and Choices

### 1. Access Your Data
You can view and access your data within the app at any time.

### 2. Delete Your Data
You can delete specific content (notes, highlights, reading plans) within the app. To delete all your data:
- Go to Settings → Profile → Delete Account
- Or email us at dailydewbible@gmail.com

### 3. Export Your Data
To request a copy of your data, email us at dailydewbible@gmail.com. We will provide your data in a portable format within 30 days.

### 4. Opt-Out of Analytics
You can disable analytics tracking in Settings → Privacy (if available in your region).

### 5. Manage Notifications
You can enable or disable push notifications in Settings → Notifications.
